About Us ☰

Track Your Progress

The Student College Preparation (SCP) database is a resourceful and informative towards one who’s interested in furthering their education. This site provides informative resources ranging from providing links with scholarship opportunities, learning how to apply for Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) or Washington Application for State Financial Aid (WAFSA), selecting calendars to mark important dates, and creating to To-Do lists to track your progress.
We hope to equipt and prepare students to be organized, educated, resourceful.

Student Click Me!

A place to gather most of the information colleges ask for and create fast access. About me will take you to a form where you can start adding personal statement ideas and include notes where you can add other info. 'Scores’ will be a place to add your scores and see if they are on track to college for example SAT/ACT and AP scores.

Preparation Click Me!

Personalize your calendars and notes from a compiled list. This will help organize important dates and setting reminders saved to your drive. Another feature you can create is a “To-Do” list which will keep you on track on items you need to complete.
